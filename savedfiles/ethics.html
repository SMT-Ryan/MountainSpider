SMT is committed to being an honest business partner

At SMT, our ethics are the cornerstone of everything we do.  When you become an SMT customer, you can be certain that you are partnering with an organization that is truly interested in the success of your business.  While many organizations claim that honesty, integrity and character are important, at SMT, ethics are at the foundation of our mission statement and embedded in the company DNA.  Our processes, contracts and billings are all designed to implement the ethical vision of the organization.  This is accomplished in a variety of ways:

  • Cost Certainty - As a WebCrescendo customer you have access to ALL features of the environment.  As new features and capabilities are added, you have complete access to these features at no additional cost.
  • Fixed Price Projects - SMT provides fixed bid pricing on all project activities we perform. You will never receive an unexpected invoice form SMT.
  • Invoice at Project Completion - AT SMT, we (usually) bill all projects at the end of the project.  We only get paid when you are satisfied with the completeness and quality of the work performed.
  • Contract Renewal Price Guarantees -SMT will not only provide pricing for the current contract, we will also guarantee our pricing for the contract renewal.  You can be assured that transparency is a priority in all that we do.
