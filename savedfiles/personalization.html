Personalized Websites

Silicon Mountain Technologies believes that your company's website should be as unique as you are. Our Web Crescendo application was specifically designed to empower website managers with the tools they need to distinguish themselves from their competitors. Web Crescendo takes personalization to new heights by allowing businesses to not only distinguish their website from the competition, but to also segment to certain user classifications (new Vs returning customers), and even personalize at the individual level (Jane Doe Vs John Smith). In a time when most companies are asking "how can we do more with less", Web Crescendo delivers with a personalized solution. Personalization gives your audience what they want, in a way that is appealing to them.
